About

After successfully owning and running New Marigold Hong Kong Dining in Doncaster with an excellent reputation and great support by loyal customers. My husband, (Yili) and I (Juan) have recently opened "Yili" a vibrant new restaurant located in Cairo, Egypt. We aim to deliver delicious traditional Chinese cuisine using recipes inspired from various regions throughout the country.

At Yilis we will continue to operate to high standards, producing food with passion and delivering this to all our new and existing customers within our amazing new space. Looking forward to seeing you soon!
